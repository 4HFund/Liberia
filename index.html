Why We're Going

We are going to listen, learn, and help tell their story.

The Free Methodist Church in Liberia is building more than congregations. Liberian leaders are developing churches, training pastors, strengthening communities, and cultivating a 100-acre property centered on cacao, animals, opportunity, and a vision for the future.

Pastor Bobby and Sidney will travel as ministry partners and storytellers. Through interviews, worship, teaching, photography, and documentary filmmaking, they will help preserve and share what Liberian Christians are already building.

“Faith is being planted in people, and hope is being planted in the ground.”
