The Foundery Church · Liberia Documentary Mission

Help us tell what God is growing.

Pastor Bobby and Sidney are traveling to Liberia as ministry partners, listeners, and storytellers. Together, they will create a modern documentary centered on Liberian churches, leaders, worship, community life, and a growing cacao and animal development project.

Support the Mission → Discover the Story

The documentary will place Liberian voices at the center and show what local church leaders are already building.

Land, faith, leadership, and a story worth carrying home.

This is more than a mission-trip fundraiser. It is a chance to listen well, build partnership, and create media that can serve the Liberian Free Methodist Church long after the cameras are packed away.

Why We Are Going

The story already belongs to Liberia.

The Free Methodist Church in Liberia is strengthening congregations, training pastors, serving families, and developing land with a vision for long-term opportunity. The planned documentary will focus on the people carrying that work forward.

Pastor Bobby and Sidney will capture interviews, worship, teaching, agriculture, community life, and the vision behind the cacao and animal development. The goal is not to make themselves the story, but to help Liberian leaders tell it in their own words.

“Faith is being planted in people, and hope is being planted in the ground.”
